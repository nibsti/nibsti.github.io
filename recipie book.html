Scrambled Eggs

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2 or 3
Butter 1 Tbsp.

Step 1: Melt the butter in a pan over meduim heat.
Step 2:While butter is melting, crack eggs into a bowl and mix with a fork until white and yolk is combined.
Step 3:Once butter is melted, pour eggs onto the pan, allowing a small layer to solidify at the bottom.
Step 4:Mix up the eggs in the pan until they look scrambled.
Step 5:Put on a plate and serve with salt, pepper, ketchup, or hot sauce to taste.

Birthday Cake

  • Time: 90 minutes
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4
Butter 3 Tbsp.
Whole Milk 2 Cups
Cake & Pastry Flour 1 1/2 Cups
Granulated Sugar 1 Cup

Step 1:Preheat oven to 350 º F. Mix it all in the biggest bowl you have.
Step 2:Beat for 2 minutes or until well mixed and small bubbles are visible.
Step 3:Pour in a cake pan and bake for 35 to 45 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.

Ants in Mud

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Raisings 1/2 Cup
Chocolate Pudding 1 Cup
Oreo Cookies 5
Gummy Worms 10 or so

Step 1: Mix everything except the Oreos.
Step 2:Remove the cream from the Oreos and either eat it or get rid of it somehow. Crush up the cookies with a rolling pin.
Step 3:Put Oreos on top of mixture once in bowls. Scare your mom with the worms.
